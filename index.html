Skandagiri Hills

Skandagiri, also known as Kalavara Durga, is famous for its early morning trek and breathtaking sunrise views above the clouds, attracting nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Mandaragi Hills

Mandaragiri Hills, also called Basadi Betta, is known for its serene Jain temple shaped like a peacock feather dome and panoramic views surrounding lakes and landscapes..

Nethravathi Hills

Nethravathi Hills is celebrated for its scenic trekking trails and mesmerizing sunsets, offering sweeping views of lush valleys offering sweeping views of lush valleys and distant mountain ranges.
